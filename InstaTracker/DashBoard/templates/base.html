This is a website using to keep track of the followers and followings of your instagram account.
In the home page dashboard, the numbers of the followers and followings, the name of the account, and the profile icon image(sometime cannot load) will be represented first.
For the second row, the cards represents the names of the accounts that come or leave specific relationship with you.
The backend system will refer to the data of your account at your first login to compare the differences.
Hope you enjoy your time. XD

ps. Once one of your follower or following change their account name, it may be counted as one mutation.
